Software Engineering Project Management

Software Engineering Project Management involves planning, organizing, and controlling software development projects. It includes activities like defining project scope, scheduling tasks, allocating resources, managing risks, facilitating communication, and ensuring quality. Effective project management is crucial for delivering software products within time and budget constraints while meeting customer requirements.
